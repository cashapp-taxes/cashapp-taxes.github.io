Understanding Taxable Income

When you are using Cash App Taxes, the nature of the transaction decides whether it is taxable or not.

 

Business Transactions: By using Cash App taxes, if you receive payment for goods and services such as freelance work, business sales, etc., this may be considered a business transaction and it is taxable. The IRS wants you to report all the income.

Personal Transaction: By using Cash App taxes, if you receive payment for personal use such as eating out or dividing the rent, etc., this may not be considered taxable income.
